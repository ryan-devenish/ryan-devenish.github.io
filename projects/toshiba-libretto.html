Toshiba Libretto

A limited edition product celebrating Toshiba's 25 years in computing.

The Libretto was designed to be different. It has two touchscreen displays that can act as separate displays or one unified screen running Windows OS. Toshiba asked Smart Design to help design proprietary software in support of key functionality for this one-of-a-kind product.

My team was tasked with desiging compelling interactions that enabled users to use the unique funcitons available in the book-like form factor. I also led the visual treatment of the entire system.
